Scientific Metadata Technician

We're looking for someone to join us as a half-time staff member to help document scientific datasets. The person hired for this position will perform documentation work for several integrated ecosystem research programs associated with Axiom Data Science and the Alaska Ocean Observing System (AOOS). The primary responsibility of this position will be to use existing documents and reports in conjunction with directly contacting scientists to create detailed, standards-compliant metadata records.

Preference will be given to applicants who can work from our office in Anchorage.

Send questions or applications to chris+technician@axiomdatascience.com. We are accepting applications for this position through September 8th.
