Axiom is an employee and life-balance focused technology and informatics firm with great mission - making scientific data public, discoverable and accessible. We specialize in developing data management systems, intuitive web-based data portals and scalable solutions for organizing and analyzing large amounts of complex heterogeneous data. Axiom is based in Anchorage, AK and has satellite offices in Fairbanks, AK, Portland, OR and North Kingstown, RI.
